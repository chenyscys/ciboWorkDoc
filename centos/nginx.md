#### 配置目录

cd /app/soft/nginx/conf/conf.d

命令

* 启动

/app/soft/nginx/sbin/nginx 

* 重启

/app/soft/nginx/sbin/nginx -s reload

* 关闭

/app/soft/nginx/sbin/nginx -s stop
